THE STUDY PLAN

16-Week Operating Protocol · One Chapter Per Session
Most people treat a book like a movie — consume it once, file it, move on. That is why most people have the same conversations about money, opportunity, and potential for decades without the variables changing. This plan is different. It is built for people who are willing to slow down enough to actually install something.
A · Pre-Load
Read & analyze the chapter several days before the session.
B · Signal
One strong reader voices the chapter aloud — with feeling.
C · Friction
All members discuss freely. Challenge every assumption.
D · Capture
Write down every idea your own mind generates. Every one.

Phase 04 · Integration — Week 16
WK16

The Final Audit
Poverty. Criticism. Ill health. Loss of love. Old age. Death. Hill's final chapter doesn't add a new principle — it removes the interference blocking all thirteen. This is the system self-test. Run it honestly.
The Operating Commitment
→Pre-load: Read the chapter 3+ days before your session. Mark every line that activates resistance or recognition.
→Show up prepared: A club without preparation is a social call. Preparation is the minimum entry standard.
→Capture everything: Write down every idea the discussion generates in your own mind. Not the book's ideas — yours. Those are the output.
→Apply within 72 hours: The principle discussed must touch your real work within three days of the session or the installation fails.
Sixteen weeks is not a long time. It is long enough to either build a new operating system or prove, once again, that you prefer the comfort of the old one. The plan doesn't judge either choice. It just keeps running.
