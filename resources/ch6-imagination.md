# Chapter 6: Imagination — The Workshop of the Mind

## Two Forms

Hill identifies two types of imagination:

**Synthetic Imagination:** Rearranges existing ideas, concepts, and plans into new combinations. Works with material already in the mind.

**Creative Imagination:** The faculty through which hunches, inspirations, and intuitions are received. Connected to the "Infinite Intelligence" — the subconscious bank.

## The Workshop

Every plan, invention, and fortune first existed as an imaginative concept. The imagination is the workshop where old ideas are combined into new ones. Hill calls it the most fascinating and mysterious of the 13 principles.

## Engineering Translation

Synthetic = combinatorial search through known patterns. Creative = receiving from the subconscious/network. Both are trainable operations.
