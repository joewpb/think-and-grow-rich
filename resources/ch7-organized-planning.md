# Chapter 7: Organized Planning — The Crystallization of Desire into Action

## The Alliance Principle

No individual achieves lasting success alone. Hill requires a "Master Mind" alliance — a coordinated group working toward a common goal. The alliance's collective intelligence exceeds the sum of its parts.

## The Plan

A plan must be practical and detailed. Hill provides the structure for forming an alliance: identify what you need, who has it, and how you'll exchange value. Leaders don't need to know everything — they need to *organize* those who do.

## Engineering Translation

Organized Planning = system architecture. The Master Mind is the processor; your plan is the instruction set. No plan survives first contact with reality — iterate.
