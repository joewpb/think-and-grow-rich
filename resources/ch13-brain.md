# Chapter 13: The Brain — A Broadcasting and Receiving Station

## The Network

Hill describes the brain as a broadcasting and receiving station for the vibration of thought. Through the principle of telepathy (as he termed it), the brain can pick up thoughts, ideas, and impulses from other brains — especially those tuned to the same frequency.

## The Master Mind Connection

This mechanism is how the Master Mind works at a subconscious level. Harmonious coordination creates a shared frequency that amplifies the group's collective receiving capacity.

## Engineering Translation

Brain = transceiver with tunable frequency. Your dominant thoughts set your broadcast frequency. The Master Mind is a phased array — multiple transceivers coordinated for higher gain and lower noise.
