Chapter 04 · Specialized Knowledge · Worksheet

SPECIALIZED KNOWLEDGE — LEVERAGE AUDIT

Principle: Personal Experiences or Observations · josie.ai Study Club
Hill's most provocative move in this chapter: 'knowledge is only potential power.' General education, untranslated into organized action toward a definite end, produces nothing. Most people confuse acquiring knowledge with building leverage. They are not the same operation.
Fields — Complete Before or During Session
My Core Specialization
State it in one precise sentence: what do you know how to do that creates specific, monetizable value that most people cannot replicate?
Knowledge Gaps vs. My Definite Want
List the 3-5 areas of specialized knowledge you currently lack that stand between you and your definite want.
My Knowledge Alliance (The Buttons)
Who in your current network fills each gap above? For gaps with no one assigned, name the type of person you need to find.
This Quarter's Learning Investment
What specific course, mentor, or resource are you committing to in the next 90 days to acquire a specific skill?
Knowledge I Have That I'm Not Deploying
What do you already know that you're not organizing into action? This is often more valuable than what you're still trying to learn.
Session Checklist
I can state my specialization in one sentence
I identified my top 3 knowledge gaps
I have at least one person assigned to each gap
I have a scheduled learning investment for this quarter
This worksheet is yours. The answers that matter most are the ones that make you pause. If nothing made you pause, you answered too quickly.
