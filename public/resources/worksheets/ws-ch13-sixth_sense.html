THE SIXTH SENSE — MASTERY SELF-ASSESSMENT

Principle: The Door to the Temple of Wisdom · josie.ai Study Club
Hill was reluctant to write this chapter. He held it for decades. The Sixth Sense is the capstone — the Creative Imagination operating at full power, accessible only after the first twelve principles have been genuinely mastered. It cannot be demanded. It must be developed. And it announces itself not as a grand revelation but as a hunch you finally learn to trust.
Fields — Complete Before or During Session
My Invisible Council
Name 5 people — historical or living — you would convene nightly to build their qualities into your character. Name the specific quality you'd request from each.
Hunch Log
Recall 3 recent instances where you received a strong intuition. Did you act on it? What happened? What pattern do you notice?
16-Week Principle Mastery Audit
Rate each of the 13 principles: 1 (aware), 2 (understood), 3 (practicing), 4 (installed), 5 (mastered). Be brutal. Total your score.
The Principle Most Demanding Deeper Work
Based on your audit, which principle — if you genuinely mastered it — would produce the largest shift in your results?
The Commitment Forward
This is not the end of 16 weeks. It is the beginning of the second cycle. What specific change in your operating protocol will you make for the next 16 weeks?
Session Checklist
I built my Invisible Council with specific names and specific requested qualities
I completed the 16-week mastery audit for all 13 principles honestly
I identified the principle demanding the most work
I have a written commitment for what changes in the next cycle
This worksheet is yours. The answers that matter most are the ones that make you pause. If nothing made you pause, you answered too quickly.
