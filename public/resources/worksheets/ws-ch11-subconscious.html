SUBCONSCIOUS MIND — INPUT INVENTORY

Principle: The Connecting Link · josie.ai Study Club
The subconscious never sleeps, never evaluates, and never takes a day off. It executes on whatever input it receives — whether you feed it deliberately or let the environment feed it by default. Seven major positive emotions. Seven major negative emotions. One will dominate. You choose, or the environment chooses for you.
Fields — Complete Before or During Session
Current Dominant Emotional State
Name the emotion that has been most present in your mind this week. Trace where it came from.
Deliberate Inputs (What I'm Planting)
List every practice, repetition, or statement you currently use to consciously feed your subconscious positive, goal-aligned content.
Environmental Inputs (What's Being Planted Without My Permission)
List the media, conversations, environments, and relationships that are currently feeding your subconscious content you didn't choose.
The Displacement Plan
For the top two uninvited inputs above, name what you will deliberately plant to displace them.
7 Positive Emotions: My Current Access
Rate your current access to each: Desire / Faith / Love / Sex / Enthusiasm / Romance / Hope. Which is weakest? What would activate it?
Session Checklist
I identified my current dominant emotion honestly
I listed both deliberate and environmental inputs to my subconscious
I have a displacement plan for at least 2 uninvited inputs
I understand why emotion is the required carrier for subconscious influence
This worksheet is yours. The answers that matter most are the ones that make you pause. If nothing made you pause, you answered too quickly.
