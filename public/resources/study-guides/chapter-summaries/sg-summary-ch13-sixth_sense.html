THE SIXTH SENSE

The Door to the Temple of Wisdom · josie.ai Study Club
"The thirteenth principle is known as the sixth sense, through which Infinite Intelligence may, and will, communicate voluntarily, without any effort from, or demands by, the individual."
Chapter Overview
Hill held this chapter for decades before writing it. The Sixth Sense is the Creative Imagination operating at full capacity — accessible only after genuine mastery of the first twelve principles. It cannot be demanded, forced, or accessed on a schedule. It arrives as hunches, inspired ideas, and direct knowing that precede rational justification. Hill describes his practice of nightly meetings with imaginary versions of great historical figures — Lincoln, Edison, Napoleon, Emerson, Darwin — as the method he used to develop the faculty over years of deliberate practice.
Key Concepts at a Glance
Accessibility Requires the Prior Twelve
The Sixth Sense is not a standalone faculty. It is the capstone — the Creative Imagination fully operational — which becomes accessible only when the other twelve principles have been genuinely installed, not merely read about.
The Invisible Counselors
Hill's practice: nightly imaginary meetings with historical figures, deliberately cultivating their specific qualities — Lincoln's fairness, Edison's tenacity, Napoleon's decisiveness — through sustained autosuggestion.
Hunches as Data
The Sixth Sense delivers through hunches, inspirations, and direct knowing. Hill says these must be acted on — not analyzed to death. The faculty develops through use and atrophies through dismissal.
Warning Function
Hill describes instances where the Sixth Sense warned him away from danger — people or situations that later proved harmful. He treats this as one of its most practical applications.
Mastery vs. Knowledge
Reading about the Sixth Sense produces no access to it. Mastering — actually installing and operating — the prior twelve principles over sustained time is the only path. Hill is explicit: this chapter is the output of a long process, not a shortcut to it.
Read this before the session. Come with the chapter already in your system — the discussion is where the installation happens, not the reading. Passive familiarity with these ideas produces no results. Application does.
