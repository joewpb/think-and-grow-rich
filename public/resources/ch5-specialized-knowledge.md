# Chapter 5: Specialized Knowledge — Personal Experiences or Observations

## General vs. Specialized

General knowledge is dead — it informs but doesn't produce. Specialized knowledge organized into a definite plan of action is what yields results. Hill distinguishes the scholar (accumulator) from the producer (organizer).

## The Organization Principle

Knowledge is not power. Knowledge *organized and intelligently directed* is power. Most millionaires Hill studied had no more than average general education — they won through focused specialization and team-based intelligence.

## Engineering Translation

Specialized knowledge = compiled expertise. Raw data is not value. Compiled, organized, and executed knowledge is. Surround yourself with people who know what you don't.
