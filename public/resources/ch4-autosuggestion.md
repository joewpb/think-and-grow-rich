# Chapter 4: Auto-Suggestion — The Medium for Influencing the Subconscious Mind

## The Bridge

Auto-suggestion is the bridge between the conscious and the subconscious. Every thought reaching the subconscious is stamped with the intensity of emotion behind it. Hill prescribes reading your desire statement aloud with *feeling* — the emotion is the carrier signal.

## The Lock

The subconscious accepts only what reaches it with sufficient emotional charge. Neutral or weak repetitions never break through. The system requires *conviction felt* before *conviction real*.

## Engineering Translation

Auto-suggestion = the write-head to your subconscious storage. Volume and emotional amplitude determine whether the write succeeds.
