# Chapter 12: The Subconscious Mind — The Connecting Link

## The Bank

The subconscious is the filing cabinet of every thought impulse reaching the conscious mind. It works day and night — accepting, filing, and acting on the dominant thoughts you feed it. It cannot distinguish between constructive and destructive impulses.

## The Autopilot

The subconscious translates desire into its physical equivalent automatically — but only after the desire reaches it with sufficient emotional charge. This is why repetition with feeling (auto-suggestion) is the essential transmission mechanism.

## Engineering Translation

Subconscious = the always-on compiler. Input: emotionally-charged dominant thoughts. Output: behavior and circumstances aligned to that input. Garbage in, garbage out — but persistent garbage in produces permanent compile.
