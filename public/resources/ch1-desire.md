# Chapter 1-2: Desire — The Starting Point of All Achievement

## The Six-Step Method

Hill presents a precise, mechanical process for turning a wish into a burning desire:

**Step 1:** Fix in your mind the *exact* amount you desire. Be specific — "a lot of money" is not a target.

**Step 2:** Determine exactly what you will *give* in return. You don't get something for nothing.

**Step 3:** Establish a definite date by which you will possess it.

**Step 4:** Create a definite plan and begin *immediately* — ready or not.

**Step 5:** Write a clear, concise statement combining 1-4: the amount, what you'll give, the date, and your plan.

**Step 6:** Read this statement aloud twice daily — once before bed, once after rising. See, feel, and *believe* yourself already in possession.

## Key Insight

A vague wish and a burning desire are not the same thing. The difference is a number, a date, a price, and a plan written down. This chapter is the factory floor — everything else in the book runs on the engine assembled here.

## Engineering Translation

Desire = an applied force with measurable parameters. Without parameters it's ambient noise.
