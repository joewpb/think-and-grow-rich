# Chapter 10: Power of the Master Mind — The Driving Force

## Definition

The Master Mind is the coordination of knowledge and effort between two or more people who work toward a definite purpose in a spirit of harmony. No individual has enough knowledge or experience to succeed alone.

## The Third Mind

When two minds coordinate in harmony, a third, invisible mind is created — a psychic faculty that can tap into resources neither individual possesses alone. This is the engine behind every great fortune Hill studied.

## Engineering Translation

Master Mind = a distributed intelligence network. A coordinated group operating with aligned incentives and harmonic communication produces output greater than the sum of its nodes.
