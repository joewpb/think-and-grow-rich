Daily Practice

Morning
Read Your Statement
Read your written desire statement aloud immediately upon waking, with full faith and visualization.
Morning
Plan Your Day
Organize your day around actions that move you toward your definite purpose.
Throughout
Monitor Your Thoughts
Catch negative thoughts and immediately replace them with positive affirmations.
Evening
Study & Reflect
Read one chapter or study guide section. Journal your insights and applications.
Evening
Read Your Statement Again
Read your written statement aloud once more before sleep. Let your subconscious work overnight.
